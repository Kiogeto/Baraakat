About Barakaat

Barakaat e-Hospital was established in 2015 with the goal of revolutionizing healthcare delivery through modern technology. Our vision is to provide accessible, high-quality medical care to all. The website was created to offer convenient access to our services, ensuring patients can easily manage appointments, access medical records, and receive timely healthcare information.Our mission is to deliver compassionate , high-quality healthcare that improves the well-being of our community. Founded by Dr. Amina Hassan and Dr. Ali Kamau, Barakaat e-Hospital has been at the forefront of medical innovation since 2015.
